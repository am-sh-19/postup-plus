When can I shower? −
Keep the incision dry until day 14. Sponge bathe only until sutures are removed.
Is swelling normal on day 7? +
Yes — ice 20 min several times daily and elevate the leg when resting.
When should I call the clinic? +
Fever over 101.5°F, spreading redness, sudden severe pain, or foot numbness.
How often should I walk? +
About 5 minutes every hour while awake — tap + in Today's movement or Log walk on the banner.
How do I take Percocet? +
Percocet 5/325 mg: one tablet every 6 hours with food for 3 weeks. Always eat with each dose; do not take more often than every 6 hours.
